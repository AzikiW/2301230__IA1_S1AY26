I could concentrate on my studies with confidence because the system could automatically switch off lights and appliances when not in use, keep an eye on our house for strange activity, and send notifications to my phone if something went wrong. The primary ethical issue is privacy because these systems have the potential to be hacked and can gather information about a family's everyday activities.
